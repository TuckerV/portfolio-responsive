About Me

My name is Tucker V. I am currently enrolled in a University of Utah coding bootcamp, to learn how to be a productive developer. Some of my hobbies include traveling with my wife, and watching netflix on the weekends.

Born and raised in Seattle Washington, I moved to Utah to go to BYU, where I received my bachelors degree in economics. Upon graduation I quickly realized that I wanted to work as a developer, and decided to begin learning technical skills in order to enter the workforce.

Currently I am working in IT for a technology recycling company, refurbishing computers for resale. I have a year and a half old dog named Banksy who has an instagram with almost 5000 followers.
